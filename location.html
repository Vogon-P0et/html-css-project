Where to Find Us

Beautifully Situated on The Shores of the Atlantic Ocean

The Atlantic Event Center is located between Atlantic and Neptune Beach in Jacksonville, FL. Read below to find some useful information while you're in town


If you're visiting near Neptune Beach in Jacksonville, FL, you'll be close to a mix of laid-back beaches and local spots. Neptune Beach itself has a quiet shoreline for walking or relaxing, with nearby Jacksonville Beach and Atlantic Beach adding more sand and surf options. You've got Pete's Bar, a longtime local hangout, and places like Flying Iguana for a casual meal, both just steps away.


A short drive opens up more to see. Tide Views Preserve offers kayaking along the Intracoastal Waterway, while the Theodore Roosevelt Area at Timucuan Preserve has trails and nature views. Jacksonville Beach has a pier for fishing or strolling, and Adventure Landing nearby has go-karts and water slides if you're with family. Beaches Town Center is also close for a quick stop at local shops.
